Raktabhiyan Mission!!

#28 State Mission

Blood Donation Initiative by Atul Singh, Dec 7, 2017

I am on a mission ‘Raktabhiyan’ which aims to encourage and inspire people all over the country do donate blood, clear up the minds of youth on myths related to blood donation.For this mission I am travelling to all the 28 states to donate blood and encourage people so that no lives go away because of blood non-availability. Currently, I have covered 17 states, more to go, West Bengal is the next state. The Journey started when I was in college where a friend’s mother needed blood in Delhi but she died. Since then I had in mind to do something, months later ‘Raktabhiyan’ started and it is ongoing good.I hope my mission will help disregard distance and time when someone is in need of blood.
